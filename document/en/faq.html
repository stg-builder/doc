Can games created with this tool be distributed?

It is possible regardless of whether it is paid or free.
There is no obligation to display a logo, etc.
If you want to display your logo in your game, please click here.

To create a package for distribution, please click here.

How to switch to Experimental build

To use the versions marked as experimental builds in the update information, you will need to participate in the beta.

Please open the Beta tab from the properties of the right-click menu of Steam and switch to Join Beta.
Select "None" to undo.

Experimental builds may contain bugs or rewind.

Cannot operate the pad on the tool.


This will occur if "Enable Steam Input" is turned on in the Steam client settings.
Please turn off.
